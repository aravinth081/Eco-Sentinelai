# EcoSentinel AI — Devpost Submission

## 1-Sentence Elevator Pitch
EcoSentinel AI is an autonomous smart city brain that leverages multi-agent AI to real-time monitor environmental threats and execute self-healing infrastructure protocols before a crisis occurs.

## Inspiration
Our inspiration came from the increasing frequency of urban environmental disasters that could have been mitigated if city infrastructure could "think" for itself. We wanted to move beyond simple monitoring dashboards and build a system that actually *acts*—closing valves during chemical spills or rerouting traffic during air quality spikes—operating as a true autonomous guardian of the city's health.

---

## Technical Architecture
1. **Perception**: Real-time IoT simulation pushing PM2.5, CO2, pH, and Wind Vectors to Firebase.
2. **Cognition**: A modular AI Gateway (Featherless/Antigravity) that analyzes multi-zone telemetry.
3. **Actuation**: Automated REST commands to virtual infrastructure (Valves, Grid, Drones).
4. **Interface**: A cyberpunk, high-telemetry dashboard with an integrated NLP Eco-Copilot.
